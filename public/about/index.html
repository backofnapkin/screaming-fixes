The gap between finding and fixing

Every SEO knows the feeling: you run a crawl, export the CSV, and stare at hundreds of issues. Then you open a spreadsheet, document every fix, and wait weeks (or months) for someone else to actually implement them.

I've been doing SEO for over 15 years. I've used every tool out there. They're great at telling you what's wrong. But none of them actually fix anything.

You end up with a spreadsheet of broken links, redirect chains, and missing alt text — a to-do list that sits in a queue behind "real" engineering work. Meanwhile, you're bleeding link equity and watching crawl budget go to waste.

Screaming Fixes is the integration layer I always wanted. It takes the audit data you already have and turns it into one-click fixes. No dev tickets. No waiting. No excuses.

This isn't about replacing the tools you already use. It's about closing the loop — taking action on what they find.
